# Product Strategy

## Overview
Petplaydate.AI is an AI-powered dog sociability and social wellness platform that helps owners build safe, real-world connections through compatibility matching, dog temperament analysis, and smart walk recommendations.

## Problem
- Many people struggle with loneliness and lack of real-world social connection.
- Dog owners already have a natural reason to go outside and interact, but there is no structured, intelligent platform that helps them connect safely.
- Meeting strangers can feel awkward or unsafe.
- Existing pet social apps can feel shallow or gimmicky.

## Solution
Fetch helps dog owners form safe, meaningful real-world connections by using AI to match owners and dogs based on compatibility, temperament, energy level, and social goals.

## Core Features
- AI compatibility scoring for owner and dog profiles
- “Why you matched” explanations
- Smart recommendations for walks and meetup spots
- Safety-aware suggestions for better places and times to meet
- Owner profile + dog profile system

## Why This Wins
- Clear AI use case
- Strong real-world relevance
- Emotionally relatable problem
- Can be built as a simple MVP
- Scalable into a larger community and wellness platform

## Positioning
### Do not call it
- Dog dating app

### Do call it
- Social wellness platform
- AI-powered community connection tool
- Dog sociability app
- Real-world connection app for dog owners

## SDG Alignment
- SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being
- SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
- SDG 5: Gender Equality through safer and more structured meeting environments

## Team Notes
- Keep the product framed around connection, wellness, and safety
- Avoid making the concept sound gimmicky
- Focus on meaningful interaction rather than “dating”
- Make the AI feel useful, explainable, and practical

## MVP Direction
For the hackathon MVP, prioritize:
1. Owner and dog onboarding quiz
2. Simple AI compatibility matching
3. Match explanation output
4. Suggested meetup or walking recommendation
5. Clean and simple UI prototype
