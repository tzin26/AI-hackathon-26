# PetPlayDate.ai 🐕

## AI-Powered Dog Sociability & Social Wellness Platform

PetPlayDate.ai is an AI-powered app that helps dog owners build safe, meaningful real-world connections through compatibility matching, dog temperament analysis, and smart walk recommendations.

---

## 🧠 The Problem
- People are increasingly experiencing loneliness and lack of connection
- Dog owners already have a natural opportunity to socialize, but no structured way to do so
- Meeting strangers can feel unsafe or awkward
- Existing apps lack depth, intelligence, or real-world focus

---

## 💡 Our Solution
PetPlayDate.ai transforms everyday dog walking into an opportunity for connection, wellness, and community.

Using AI, we match dog owners based on:
- Personality and social preferences
- Dog temperament and energy levels
- Activity habits and lifestyle

---

## ⚙️ Core Features
- AI compatibility matching (owner + dog)
- “Why you matched” explanations
- Smart walk and meetup recommendations
- Safety-aware location suggestions
- Dual profiles (owner + dog)
